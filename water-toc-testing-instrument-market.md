<p><p>The Water TOC Testing Instrument market encompasses various testing methods. The NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) method measures organic carbon content using infrared light, offering high sensitivity. The UV method detects TOC by measuring UV absorbance, effective for low concentrations. The Conductance method focuses on changes in electrical conductivity to assess organic matter presence. Other methods include advanced techniques like UV-Vis spectroscopy and chromatographic approaches, catering to diverse industrial needs and enhancing the accuracy and efficiency of water quality assessments.</p></p>
