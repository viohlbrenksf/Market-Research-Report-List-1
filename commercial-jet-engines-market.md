<p><p>The commercial jet engine market comprises three main types: turbojet, turbofan, and turboprop engines. Turbojet engines, known for high-speed performance, are mainly used in military aircraft but have limited applications in commercial aviation due to noise and efficiency issues. Turbofan engines are the most prevalent in commercial aviation, offering improved fuel efficiency and reduced noise, making them suitable for a variety of passenger and cargo aircraft. Turboprop engines, combining jet engine technology with propellors, excel in regional and short-haul flights due to their efficiency at lower speeds.</p></p>
