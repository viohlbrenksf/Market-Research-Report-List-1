<p><p>The fluoropolymer membrane market comprises various types, including PFSA (Perfluorosulfonic Acid), known for its high conductivity and chemical resistance; PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy), which offers thermal stability; and FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene), known for its low friction properties. ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) is praised for its high tensile strength, while PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) is widely used in membrane applications for its versatility. PCTFE (PolyChlorotrifluoroethylene) is noted for its impermeability, and other types refer to various specialty membranes tailored for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
