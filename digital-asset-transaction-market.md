<p><strong>The Digital Asset Transaction Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pay To Public Key Hash</li><li>Pay To Public Key</li><li>Pay To Script Hash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital asset transaction market types encompass various methods of facilitating transfers on blockchain networks. "Pay To Public Key Hash" (P2PKH) involves sending funds to a hashed version of a user's public key, requiring the corresponding private key to access. "Pay To Public Key" (P2PK) directly pays to a public key, requiring the receiver to sign transactions with their private key. "Pay To Script Hash" (P2SH) allows payments to be made to a hashed script, enabling complex conditions for fund retrieval, enhancing flexibility and security.</p></p>
