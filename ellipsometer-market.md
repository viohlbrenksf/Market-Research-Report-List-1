<p><strong>The Ellipsometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spectroscopic Ellipsometer</li><li>Laser Ellipsometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ellipsometer market is primarily divided into two types: Spectroscopic Ellipsometers and Laser Ellipsometers. Spectroscopic Ellipsometers utilize a broad spectrum of light to measure film thickness and optical properties across various wavelengths, making them suitable for detailed material characterization. In contrast, Laser Ellipsometers employ a single wavelength laser for high-precision measurements, focusing on thin films and surface analysis. Both types serve industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, materials science, and optics, facilitating advancements in thin-film technology and surface engineering.</p></p>
