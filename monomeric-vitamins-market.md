<p><ul><li>Fat-Soluble Vitamins</li><li>Water Soluble Vitamins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The monomeric vitamins market is categorized into fat-soluble and water-soluble vitamins. Fat-soluble vitamins, including A, D, E, and K, are stored in body fat and play crucial roles in various physiological functions, such as vision, immune response, and bone health. Water-soluble vitamins, such as B-complex and C, dissolve in water and are essential for energy production, metabolism, and overall health. Both categories are vital for dietary supplementation and contribute significantly to consumer health products and nutraceuticals.</p></p>
