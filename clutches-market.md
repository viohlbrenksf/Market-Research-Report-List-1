<p><p>The clutches market is categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and full-automatic. Manual clutches require drivers to engage and disengage the clutch using a pedal, offering greater control but demanding more skill. Semi-automatic clutches ease operation by requiring minimal driver input, often automating gear changes while still involving some manual action. Full-automatic clutches completely take over the gear-changing process, allowing drivers to focus solely on steering and acceleration, thus enhancing convenience and accessibility for various driving experiences.</p></p>
