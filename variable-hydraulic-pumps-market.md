<p><strong>The Variable Hydraulic Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Pumps</li><li>Radial Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Variable Hydraulic Pumps market consists of two main types: axial pumps and radial pumps. Axial pumps utilize a configuration where the flow axis aligns with the pump's shaft, offering efficiency and compact design, ideal for high flow applications. In contrast, radial pumps direct fluid at a right angle to the shaft, typically providing higher pressure and suited for applications requiring robust performance. Both types are vital in hydraulic systems for their adaptability and efficiency in various industrial and mobile machinery sectors.</p></p>
