<p><p>The Needle Sensor market can be categorized into two main types: Contactless Sensors and Piercing Sensors. Contactless Sensors operate without physical contact, utilizing technologies like infrared or magnetic fields to detect and measure parameters such as blood glucose levels or other vital signs. In contrast, Piercing Sensors require a small puncture in the skin to gather samples, like in traditional blood testing. Both types play crucial roles in healthcare, enhancing patient monitoring and diagnostics while varying in their approach to data collection.</p></p>
