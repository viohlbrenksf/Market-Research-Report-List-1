<p><strong>The Tile Sealer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Penetrating Sealants</li><li>Surface Sealants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tile sealer market primarily consists of two types: penetrating sealants and surface sealants. Penetrating sealants are designed to soak into porous surfaces, providing moisture resistance and protection against stains while preserving the tile's natural appearance. In contrast, surface sealants create a protective layer on top of the tile, enhancing gloss and color while serving as a barrier against dirt and spills. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining tile durability and aesthetics in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
