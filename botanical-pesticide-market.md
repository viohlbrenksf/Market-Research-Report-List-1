<p><p>The botanical pesticide market comprises various types, including phytotoxins, phytogenous insect hormones, and others. Phytotoxins are natural compounds derived from plants that can be toxic to pests, disrupting their life processes. Phytogenous insect hormones, on the other hand, mimic or interfere with the hormonal systems of insects, disrupting their growth and reproduction. Other botanical pesticides may include essential oils and plant extracts that repel or deter pests. Together, they offer environmentally friendly pest control alternatives.</p></p>
