<p><ul><li>Non-contact Temperature Monitoring System</li><li>Contact Temperature Monitoring System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temperature monitoring systems are essential in various industries for ensuring safety and efficiency. The non-contact temperature monitoring system uses infrared technology to measure surface temperatures without physical touch, making it ideal for quick assessments in environments like hospitals and food production. In contrast, contact temperature monitoring systems involve devices like thermocouples or thermistors, which require physical contact to measure temperature accurately. These systems are often used in industrial settings where precise temperature control is critical for processes and equipment.</p></p>
