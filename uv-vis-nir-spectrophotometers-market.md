<p><p>The UV-VIS-NIR spectrophotometer market is categorized mainly into single monochromator and double monochromator types. Single monochromator spectrophotometers utilize one monochromator to isolate wavelengths, offering a compact design and simplicity, suitable for routine analysis. In contrast, double monochromator systems employ two monochromators, providing enhanced sensitivity and reduced stray light, making them ideal for demanding applications where precision and accuracy are critical. These distinctions cater to varying user needs across research, industry, and quality control settings.</p></p>
