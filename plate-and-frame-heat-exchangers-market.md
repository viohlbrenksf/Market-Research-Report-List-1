<p><p>The plate and frame heat exchangers market features three primary types: gasketed, brazed, and welded heat exchangers. Gasketed heat exchangers utilize removable gaskets for flexibility in maintenance and design adjustments, making them popular in various industries. Brazed heat exchangers employ copper or nickel brazing for strong, compact designs, ideal for high-pressure applications. Welded heat exchangers, on the other hand, use welded plates for a robust construction, suitable for demanding environments and fluids, enhancing durability and efficiency.</p></p>
