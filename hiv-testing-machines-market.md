<p><strong>The HIV Testing Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapid Immunoassay Test</li><li>Rapid Molecular Diagnostic Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HIV testing machines market comprises two main types: Rapid Immunoassay Tests and Rapid Molecular Diagnostic Tests. Rapid Immunoassay Tests utilize specific antibodies to detect HIV antigens or antibodies in a sample, providing quick results typically within minutes. In contrast, Rapid Molecular Diagnostic Tests analyze the genetic material of the virus, offering high sensitivity and specificity, with results available in under an hour. Both types enable swift diagnosis, improving patient management and facilitating timely treatment and prevention measures.</p></p>
