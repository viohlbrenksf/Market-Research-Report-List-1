<p><strong>The ArF Excimer Laser Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ArF Immersion</li><li>ArF Dry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ArF excimer laser market is primarily divided into two applications: ArF immersion and ArF dry lithography. ArF immersion technology uses a water-based medium between the lens and the wafer, enhancing resolution and enabling the production of smaller features on semiconductor chips. In contrast, ArF dry lithography employs an air gap, making it suitable for larger feature sizes. Both technologies are crucial in advanced semiconductor manufacturing, allowing for the production of high-performance integrated circuits essential for modern electronics.</p></p>
