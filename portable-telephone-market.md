<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>DECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable telephone market comprises various types, including analog and DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) systems. Analog phones utilize traditional radio frequency signals to transmit voice, offering simple functionality and limited features. In contrast, DECT technology provides a digital alternative with enhanced call clarity, security, and features like multiple handset support and mobility within larger areas. DECT systems are widely used in homes and offices due to their superior audio quality and reduced interference compared to analog phones.</p></p>
