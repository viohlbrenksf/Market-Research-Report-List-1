<p><ul><li>High Dose Rate</li><li>Low Dose Rate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brachytherapy equipment is categorized into high dose rate (HDR) and low dose rate (LDR) markets. HDR brachytherapy delivers a concentrated dose of radiation directly to the tumor in short bursts, allowing for precision and reduced exposure to surrounding healthy tissues. Conversely, LDR brachytherapy involves the continuous delivery of radiation over an extended period using radioactive seeds, providing a prolonged therapeutic effect. Both types are essential in treating various cancers, with distinct applications based on tumor characteristics and patient needs.</p></p>
