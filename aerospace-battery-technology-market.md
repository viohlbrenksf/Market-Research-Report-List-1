<p><p>The aerospace battery technology market encompasses various types, each with distinct characteristics. Nickel-Cadmium (Ni-Cd) batteries are known for their reliability and high discharge rates, often used in older aircraft systems. Lead Acid batteries are cost-effective but heavier, commonly utilized for backup power. Lithium-Ion batteries have gained popularity due to their high energy density, lighter weight, and longer life cycle, making them ideal for modern aerospace applications. The market continues to evolve as advancements enhance efficiency and safety standards.</p></p>
