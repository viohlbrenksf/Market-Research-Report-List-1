<p><ul><li>MERV 7 and Below</li><li>MERV 8</li><li>MERV 9</li><li>MERV 10</li><li>MERV 11</li><li>MERV 12</li><li>MERV 13</li><li>MERV 14 and Above</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pleated filters are categorized by their Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value (MERV) ratings, which indicate their ability to capture airborne particles. MERV 7 and below offer basic filtration for larger particles. MERV 8 and 9 are suitable for residential use, capturing mold spores and pet dander. MERV 10 and 11 provide improved filtration for finer particulates. MERV 12 and 13 are effective for allergens like pollen, while MERV 14 and above target the smallest particles, suitable for industrial or healthcare settings with high air quality demands.</p></p>
