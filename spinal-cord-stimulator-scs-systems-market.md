<p><p>Spinal Cord Stimulator (SCS) systems are classified into two main types: rechargeable and non-rechargeable. Rechargeable SCS systems feature batteries that can be recharged by the patient, offering longer-lasting energy and eliminating the need for frequent replacements. Non-rechargeable systems, on the other hand, come with fixed batteries that require surgical replacement after a specific lifespan. The choice between the two is influenced by factors like patient lifestyle, cost considerations, and the expected duration of treatment.</p></p>
