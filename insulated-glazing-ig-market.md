<p><p>Insulated Glazing (IG) consists of multiple glass panes separated by a spacer, creating an air or gas-filled space to enhance thermal and acoustic insulation. There is also a growing preference for triple glazing over double glazing, offering even better thermal performance in extreme climates. Additionally, the integration of smart technologies, such as electrochromic and thermochromic glazing, is gaining traction, making windows responsive to environmental conditions.</p><p>The Insulated Glazing (IG) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.1% during the forecast period, reflecting strong demand across various sectors.
