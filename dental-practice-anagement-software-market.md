<p><p>Dental Practice Management Software (DPMS) is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based DPMS allows dentists to access their data remotely via the internet, offering flexibility and automatic updates, which can enhance collaboration and reduce IT maintenance costs. In contrast, on-premise DPMS involves installing software on local servers, providing greater control over data security and compliance but necessitating more resources for maintenance and updates. Both options cater to different operational needs and preferences in dental practices.</p></p>
