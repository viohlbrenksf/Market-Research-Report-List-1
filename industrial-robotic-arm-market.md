<p><p>The industrial robotic arm market comprises several types of robots, each designed for specific applications. Articulated robots feature rotating joints for a wide range of motion, ideal for complex tasks. Parallel robots, with their fixed bases and movable arms, excel in precision and speed for assembly operations. SCARA robots, characterized by their horizontal arm configuration, are perfect for tasks requiring swift vertical movements. Cylindrical robots combine rotational and linear motions, while Cartesian robots operate within a rectangular coordinate system, suitable for straightforward motions and tasks.</p></p>
